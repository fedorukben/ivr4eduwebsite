Getting Started with VR in Education

  • Ensure proper setup of VR equipment in your classroom
  • Establish clear guidelines for VR usage
  • Consider time management for VR sessions
  • Plan for technical troubleshooting

Best Practices

  • Start with short VR sessions (15-20 minutes)
  • Have clear learning objectives for each VR activity
  • Provide alternative activities for students who may experience motion sickness
  • Include reflection and discussion time after VR experiences
